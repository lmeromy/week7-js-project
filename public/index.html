60 Years of Atmospheric Carbon Dioxide (CO2)

What is the Keeling Curve? In 1958, Charles David Keeling began measuring atmospheric CO2 at the Mauna Loa Observatory in Hawaii.

It is now the oldest, continuous record of CO2, and despite seasonal fluctuation, it shows a clearly increasing trend.
